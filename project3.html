Story

Everyday, hospitals meet to discuss the health and safety of the different departments within the hospital. The Daily Safety Check is a meeting where all this takes place. Each department will report using certain termonology that allows the scribe to effeciently record their reports.

The Companion App is a tool to assist in the engagement of attendees and help the scribes collect reports. Users will be prompted to enter their reports before the meeting, giving the scribe more time to validate their reports. The focus of this application is to assist meeting process and create a more effecient flow.



Challenges

~ Prompting the user to be an active attendee.
~ Allow users to interact with live information.
~ Instruct the users on how to report.
~ Provide easy instructions for new attendees.


Solutions

A data visualization that give the user a visual for the harms, good catches, risk/others and serious safety events, generated from the last serious event. A timeline allows the user to view the past and present events that have been recorded. Clear and precise information allows for the user to stay informed before and after the meeting.





Sidebars (meetings between departments) are presented in a scrollable feed. They also allow the user to include themselves in specific sidebars. The notes page includes all notes uploaded after the DSC meeting. The user will have a chance to add or edit any of the notes with the use of an overlay. When the user is finished editing the note, they can either publish their work for other departments to view.

Goals

The Companion App is a tool that allows the attendees to be active members of the meeting with clear information and instruction. The goal is to keep the members engaged in the meeting and make the meeting progress faster.

Story

Brand identity is a key component to any company. It is the first thing that potential customers see. It is the mark that draws them in to ask questions, think, and make their next move. Branding is a style that a company identifies itself with.

Apenotiq is a brand and a style on its own. It stands for captivating the primitive eye in all of us. Apenotiq looks to imitate color, texture, pattern, form, space, and shapes, that appear to be regular to us.

It is not limited by a medium, because it is a brand that encompasses all design, art and functionality.

Apenotiq is the illusive captivity of creation that imitates our primitive insticts of adaption and expression



Challenges

~ Creating a style that can display the meaning for Apenotiq.
~ Exploring mediums the push the limits of this style.
~ Discovering innovative ways to imitate the human expression and adaptability.


Solutions

The first part of the process is to identify examples of the brand. Breaking down root words and definitions to build a visual. Then further breaking down the details of the visuals to explore its limits. There is no wrong answer for this stage of the process, the main objective is to explore.





Once the visuals have been broken down and we have explored the details that make up the visual, the next step is to explore color, space, texture and form.

How does our visual work in the physical?




We begin to ask more and more questions:

Where does the visual live?

What sustains the visual to be compeling?

How do the details create the visual?

How can we manipulate the details to create something different?

How does color, space, shape, texture, and form, affect the visual?





The Exploration Continues

As we continue to explore the visual style and the brand identity, we will continue to ask questions, push limits, and captivate onlookers. Our goal is to find the imperfection that will differentiate our brand from others that already exist. In the end, the only thing left will be the visual and the space it will occupy

What is Energy?

Energy is a phenomanon that is expressed in many different ways around and within the Earth.

However, many people are unaware of the energy around them. Without this understanding, its true importance may never be understood.



Solution

With the use of an Interactive Installation, we can expose our community to the energy that flows throughout the world.



Experience Design

Programs used: Illustrator, After Effects, and Cinema4D.



Kiosk






The main Interaction point for the user. They will perform different types on gestures to progress through the experience



Curved Display






The large display will showcase the information for the user. The curve of the screen creates an enclosed space that represents the energy that surrounds us.



Curved Display UI

The source of information

Redefined Experience

As we continue to explore this idea of energy, we must continue to think about the experience of our installation.

Removing the kiosk and adding stereo gesture camera technology, we create an experience that engages the users full potential






Creative Blasterx Senz3d






With the Gesture Sensing technology, we can create a new experience for the users that allows them to interact with the installation with just a wave of the hand.

This feature allows for a range on interactive possibilities from greeting the installation with a wave to voice control.



With ENR-G, we can explore to constant flow around us. We can experience energy

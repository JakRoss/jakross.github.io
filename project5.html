Story

Everyday, people are scrolling the internet feeds for the latest highlights of their favorite players and teams. With over 100 different sports around the world, its hard to stay up-to-date wth all of them at once. If a fan wants to see certain player or team, they have to go through a grooling process of searching for the right sources. After the find the source, they still have to make sure it's the content they want.

The Reel is an exclusive highlight source for every fans desires. Now, fans will have the ability to view all their favorite sports highlights. Fans can search for their desired sport and follow their reels, create a list of favorites and even share reels with friends on other social media plateforms.

Every fan deserves a chance to root for their team. With the Reel, those chances are endless.



Challenges

~ Providing a service that allows users to view a variety of sports.
~ Allowing users to personalize their feeds.
~ Staying up-to-date with favorite teams and players.
~ Developing a filter system for easier searches.


Solutions

A feed that flows smoothly into videos. Swiping left or right slide the current clip away and reveal the clip underneath. The goal for this interaction is to get the user to endlessly swipe for content.



Fans can save clips and share them to other plateforms. This creates a personal feel for each user and also gives them an outlet to share their saved clips with friends. It also serves as a good marketing strategy to expand the visability of the app for new users to join.



Teams have their own highlight feeds fans can follow. the users can also search by team, country, sport, players, or leagues. this allows them to get more specific in their searches and find the content they are looking for.



Goals

The Reel provides it's users with an endless stream of personalized content. Our goal was to create an application the allows users to stay up-to-date with all their favorite teams. The functionality of the application allows for easy navigation, cross-plateform sharing, and an engaging UI that users will enjoy everytime they open the app.

Story

Whenever we suffer from an injury, the recovery process is the most important part to getting back to our regular lifestyles. Not only is recovery a physical process, it is a mental process as well. Everyday can be a struggle to get motivated and fight the pain of your injury, this takes a tole on the patient and makes recovery harder.

We are in the age where technology can be used to improve our lifestyles. Why not take advantage of these advances and apply them to physical therapy?

Using Augmented Reality (AR) technology and the Microsoft Hololens, TheraPlay provides a services that aims to enhance the physical therapy experience both mentally and physically. .



Users

The user persona is defined by a patient that has went through a major injury that has caused them to lose mobility and function in their body.

For this specific user they have lost the mobility in their legs and is undergoing physical therapy that will be enhanced for the Hololens.


Challenges

  • Implementing exercising into a gamified experience.
  • Developing an experience that motivates the user and pushes them to work hard.
  • Creating an adaptable AR enviornment that meets the prescribed therapy treatment.
  • Creating engaging visuals that can enhance the user experience.
  • Utilizing the Hololens technology to its fullest potential.


Solutions

The Microsoft Hololens offers a wide range of features that can be used to create an experience that engages the users whole body and mind. The headset allows the user to move freely within their environment. For interactions, this technology utilizes gesture controls to interact within the AR environment.

Gestures and Interaction:
- Air Tapping.
- Tap & Hold.
- Manipulation.
- Voice controls.
- Gaze Targeting.
- Blooming.






Research

In order to create the right experience, I conducted interviews with the RIT Athletics Training Staff to create exercise programs that could be implemented in the AR experience. During the interviews, we created a scenerio that envolved a patient working to improve the mobility in their legs. We then made a list of exercises that would be prescribed to the patient.

The next step was to understand the limits to our technology. Using Cinema4D, we created assets and exported them as .obj files to be imported into the Hololens. By placing the assets in the AR environment we were able to see whow much control we had and what we could do with those controls.





Goals

The goal for the project was to explore how technology could be used to enhance the physical therapy experience. Our focus was to utilize AR rendering and the Holonens Headset the create an experience that motivates the user and keeps them engaged in there recovery. The final result was a protoype environment that shows the potential of this technology and its influence the medical industry.

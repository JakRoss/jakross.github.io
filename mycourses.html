Problem Statement

How can myCourses be designed to fit the needs of the students and still effectively communicate the overall goal and intentions of the site?

Target Audience

RIT Students and Staff

My Role

User Interface Design

User Experience Design

Viual Design

View Process Deck

Solution

Create a design that is Quick, Efficient, and Reliable. Limit the number of pages of the site and the overall number of times the user has in click the mouse or tap the screen. Create a design that will allow the professor to edit and make changes to class content.

    Home Page

  1. Reliable and clear navagation for easy to use directing and searching.
  2. Heading provides notifications for campus events and news, creating a constant connection throughout campus.
  3. A course calender that shows all upcomming due dates and personal due dates edited by the student.
  4. Quick Notifications and Updates that keep the student aware of school updates and current courses notifications.
  5. Campus news section allows all important campus new to be viewed quickly by the students.
  6. Google Apps allows the students to connedt to their google account and accessany documents or email from their account.
  7. Program Reqironments allows students to see their progress within their academic career.
  8. Quick Links gives student the oppurtunity to save external links to their home page to create quick access to external sites.

    Course Page

  1. Heading that includes any media content or announcment created for the course.
  2. Course updates provide quick information about courses material and resources.
  3. Professor Profile allows student to see any contact information regarding the current course professor.
  4. The content and dropox section allows users to view all course content and make assignment submissions. The user also has the ability to end emails directly from the course page.
  5. Grades are easily visiable for students from the course page. allowing them to check any grade updates from the professor.
  6. Easly access to the Course description allocates few reliable information ready for the student to access.
  7. Without leaving the page the student has to ability to view any discussion posts relating to the courses
  8. Early Alert information is provided for the student to access at anytime.

Final Goals

The design resulted in pages that contained much of the content that was specific to the purpose of the page. Providing the user with all of the major content on page allows for quick and efficient access that will satisfy their needs. The visual design of the course page makes it so the professor view is highly adaptable without many changes to the interface.

Get to know me.

I'm Jaymee Rosser, a Product Designer who enjoys creating experiences that benefit and influence users. Much of my inspiration comes from Art, Research and Society. I specialize in UI UX, Data Visualization, Motion Graphics, 3D digital design and Research. I also have a passion for Branding and Illustration.

In my spare time, I like to read, play basketball, and spend time with friends and family. If I'm lucky, I can do some of those at the same time.

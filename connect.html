Story

The users of today are distracted by thousands of application being made everyday. Why not create an app that allows users to connect their favorite social media accounts into one feed.

User Persona

Our user for this application is a young adult who's life revolves around social media.

My Role

User Experience Design

Motion Graphics and Prototyping


Cross Sharing

Simple interactions are the basis for user engagement. Rotating, press and hold, and drag and drop are simple interaction that allow the user to quickly switch from scrolling through the feed to sharing your favorite posts.

Engaging

To engage the user, interaction are limited for quick and easy use. The user has the ability to quickly switch accounts at any time, making it easier to see many different accounts in one application.

Final Goals

With simplicity and cohesion, ConnectShare looks to bridge the gap between viewing your favorite Social Networks and sharing acrossed multiple plateforms. Less Button. Less Pages. More Productivity.

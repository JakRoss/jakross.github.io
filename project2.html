Story

Nursing homes all other the country are constantly looking for way to improve their quality care. This is just one of the factors that impact a homes overall star rating. These ratings are important because the higher the rating the more likely families will choose their homes for loved ones.

To assist in the improvment of quality care, the QualityStar application was developed to help track the quality of care for each nursing home. This application will allow nurses to easlily track the progress of care ranging from specific measures to specific patients.

Now, nurses and administraters can make sure they are on track to improving quality for all its patient, and focus on impriving star ratings.



Challenges

~ Providing all the quality care information for various departments to view and analyze.
~ Create engaging data visualizations that are clear and easy for all employees to understand
~ Focus on the details that will help improve quality care.
~ Create a user experience that is highly functional and easy to use.


Solutions

The biggest aspect of the solutions is the dashboard layout. This design offered many solutions to figuring out how the information was going to be divided. The home page provides the major visualization that the user will be focused on the most. These visualizations have further break downs that can be found using the side navigation.
The data visualizations are derived from the countless amounts of spreadsheets that are used to present, measure and analyze the quality measures. The visualizations were a suitable solutions for the data because it allows for the information to be translated easily so that the user can understand and analyse the information quickly and effeciently.



The overall design is intended for mainly tablet use. This technology works well with our solution because it allows for more moblity. We understand that the hospital environment is on a constant flow, our application doesn't impeed the flow and keeps the user engaged with their work.



Goals

QualityStar's focus is the improve the quality measures of nursing homes. The service provides data analytics, tracking data trends and provides information on how to improve. With an engaging design and funtionality to increase cohesion and growth, QualityStar is the tool to enhance the care of all patients.

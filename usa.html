Mission

Make browsing news events convenient, efficient, and smarter so that millennials will feel more comfortable and informed when using the app.

User Persona

Our users are mostly young adult who look for their news on the go. We are looking to appeal to a younger focus group due to research that suggest this demographic is more likely to use a mobile application for news.

My Role

User Experience Design

Motion Graphics and Prototyping

View Process Deck

Verticle Swiping Feed

Focus is on one article. Easily swipe through articles that they’re not interested in. Doesn’t overwhelm the user ***Ads can be easily placed in this style of feed and users can easily swipe past them

Minimize ‘new’ screens

Keeps the user in the feed.

Common actions and pathways.

Always at home

Options for personalization

Custom Notifications

Instantly updates user on their interests. Sleep Modes. Filter their categories.

Goals

Our goal is to give users a reoccuring reminder of the quality information that USA TODAY offers by leveraging their phone notifactions, as well as giving them the option to explore a unique article feed that can take their curiousity and interests to the next level.
